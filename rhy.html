How to Play

🎯 Goal

Tap the circles when they reach the hit zone at the bottom. The closer to the center, the higher your score!

⌨️ Controls

PC: Use keyboard keys D, F, J, K, L (or Space for center)

Mobile: Tap the buttons at the bottom of the screen

📊 Scoring

PERFECT - ±50ms = 300 points

GREAT - ±100ms = 200 points

GOOD - ±150ms = 100 points

MISS - Too late = 0 points

🔥 Combos

Hit consecutive notes to build your combo! Higher combos give bonus points.
