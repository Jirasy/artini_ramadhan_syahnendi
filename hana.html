How to Play

1

Customers Arrive

Customers walk up to the bar with their orders. Each has a patience timer — serve them before it runs out!

2

Select a Customer

Tap a customer to select them. The recipe hint will appear — it shows which ingredients you need for their drink.

3

Add Ingredients

Tap the ingredient bottles on the shelf to pour them into the glass. Watch the glass fill up!

4

Shake & Serve!

Once all ingredients are added, tap Shake to mix, then Serve to deliver. Perfect recipes earn bonus coins!

5

Level Up!

Serve enough customers to advance levels. Higher levels bring faster customers, more complex recipes, and bigger tips!

💡

Tips & Tricks

• Tap trash icon to dump a drink and start over
• Perfect drinks = 2× coins
• Build combos for extra bonuses
• 3 missed customers = Game Over!
