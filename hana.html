How to Play

1

Customers Arrive

Customers walk up to the bar with their orders displayed in a speech bubble. Each has a patience timer — serve them before it runs out!

2

Read the Recipe

Check the order bubble to see which ingredients are needed. Each cocktail has a specific recipe you must follow.

3

Add Ingredients

Tap the ingredient bottles on the shelf to add them to the glass. The glass fills up as you add each ingredient. Watch for the right amounts!

4

Shake & Serve!

Once all ingredients are added, tap the Shake button to mix, then tap Serve to deliver the drink. Perfect recipes earn bonus coins!

5

Level Up!

Serve enough customers to advance levels. Higher levels bring faster customers, more complex recipes, and bigger tips!

💡

Tips & Tricks

• Tap the trash icon to dump a drink and start over
• Perfect drinks = 2x coins
• Combos give extra bonus
• 3 missed customers = Game Over!
