HALMA

Classic Star Board Strategy Game

Halma Rules

🎯 Objective: Be the first player to move all your pieces from your starting corner (camp) into the opposite corner (target camp).

👥 Players: 2, 3, 4, or 6 players on a star-shaped board. Each player starts with 10 or 15 pieces in their home triangle.

🔄 Turns: Players take turns clockwise. On your turn, move one piece.

➡️ Step Move: Move one piece to any adjacent empty cell (6 directions on the star board).

⏭️ Jump Move: Jump over an adjacent piece (yours or opponent's) to an empty cell directly beyond it. You can chain multiple jumps in one turn!

🏠 Camp Rules:

  • Pieces in the target camp cannot leave it once entered.
  • You can move through any camp during jumps.
  • If your starting camp still has your pieces, you must move them out before making non-essential moves.

🏆 Winning: The first player to fill their target camp with all their pieces wins!

⚠️ No Blocking: You cannot permanently block an opponent by occupying their target camp cells with your own pieces without intention to leave. (In this version, camp occupation is enforced naturally.)
