How to Play

🎯 Objective: Be the last player standing by bankrupting all opponents!
🎲 Rolling: Roll two dice and move your token. If you roll doubles, roll again!
🏠 Properties: Land on an unowned property to buy it. If someone owns it, pay rent!
🏗️ Houses: Own all properties of one color to build houses (up to 5). Houses increase rent.
💰 GO: Collect $200 each time you pass GO.
🚔 Jail: Land on "Go to Jail" to go to jail. Pay $50, use a card, or roll doubles to escape.
🎴 Cards: Community Chest and Chance cards give random events!
💸 Tax: Income Tax costs $200, Luxury Tax costs $100.
🚂 Railroads: Rent increases with each railroad owned (1=$25, 2=$50, 3=$100, 4=$200).
💡 Utilities: Rent = 4x dice roll (1 utility) or 10x dice roll (2 utilities).
